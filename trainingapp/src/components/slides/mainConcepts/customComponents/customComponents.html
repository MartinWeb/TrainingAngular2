Main Concepts Custom Components - What do we know

To summarize what we have already seen about components

  • Components are directives with templates:
    • Use of template and templateUrl attributes
  • Components and Directives allow to add some behavior to your app
  • Custom components/directives are referenced in templates through their name, and you also have to declare them in order to use them
    • The selector attribute allows to give them a name
    • The directives attributes allow to declare the custom Components on which you depend
  • Our Components/Directives are parameterizables through the Property Binding mechanism
    • The properties bindings can be declared through the inputs attribute
    • Or with the @Input() annotation
  • Our Components/Directives could depend on services, and we could want specific providers for them
    • It's what the providers attribute is for
